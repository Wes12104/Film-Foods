About Our Menu Circulation


The recipes for our foods all come from Chef Andrew Rea. These recipes he creates by watching the films or TV-Shows they're from and analyzing the food he wants to make. Often he'll go through many recipes to find the most simple and most accurate recipe. We'll take these recipes and find the ones we think people will like the most. Of course there all delicious which is why we trade some foods on the menu out every month.

The foods on our menu often change as we trade them in and out of a circulation every-month. This helps provide a more diverse experience each time you come. We like to trade out our Entrees the most as we only have four on our menu. We also trade out our Share Sized Meals so people can experience our biggest and most wacky food recipes.
