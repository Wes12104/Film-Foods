About Our Buisness


Film Foods is a new restaurant chain based around serving you foods from your favorite Movies and TV-Shows. We strive to serve you the most delicious replicas of these foods in a positive dining enviornment. We want to provide an experience that you can enjoy. We train all employees to be as effecient and friendly as possible. If you have any feedback from your dining experience click the button below.



About Our Head Chef

Andrew Rea is the Head Chef and Co-Founder of Film Foods. Rea used to run the You-Tube channel "Babish Culinary Universe." On his youtube channel Rea showed viewers a clip from a movie or TV-Show featuring a food that he would re-create each week. In these videos Chef Rea showed viewers the ingredients and process to make the food. We loved the idea and decided to begin a company based around this premise with Chef Rea. He provided us with his recipes and culinary expertise in return for a majority share and a good salary.
